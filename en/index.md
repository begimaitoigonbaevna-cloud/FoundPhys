# Welcome to FoundPhys

FoundPhys is an educational physics project aimed at systematizing core topics, theories, and experiments.  
It provides resources to help students and science enthusiasts understand fundamental principles of physics and see their practical applications.  

Go to harmonic oscillations page: [Harmonic Oscillations](/en/harmonic)

## Project Goals

- Organize basic and advanced physics topics.  
- Demonstrate physical phenomena through practical examples and experiments.  
- Create a convenient online reference for students and learners.

# Mechanics

Mechanics studies the motion of bodies and the forces that cause this motion. It is divided into classical mechanics (Newton’s laws), dynamics, and statics.

**Key Concepts:**
- Force, mass, acceleration (F = ma)
- Momentum and energy
- Newton’s laws of motion
- Work and power
